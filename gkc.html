GramKalyan Center

GramKalyan Centers, GKCs will our flagship digital store in all rural areas of India connecting urban and rural economies. GKCs will act as a physical and digital connect for urban population to consume village produce directly, it will also act as a data hubs for brands connecting to rural India markets and will offer an infinite digital shopping to rural population accessing the world directly from their own friendly location and a local friendly GKC Manager.
