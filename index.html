Brief About

Gramkalyan

This consists of 2 cross Platform applications made for our customers and merchants wanting to sell their goods online in a hyperlocal environment.

Buyer App

The app on customers end, would have basic features that enable him to place an order and keep track of all the orders he’s previously placed. The user interface would be really easy to use and we have tried to keep the number of available operations minimum on the user end, as it can be intimidating for many people to start using a new app that bombards them with a lot of features and functionalities at once. (And in this case since the primary users are expected to be young adults to middle aged people, with a significant part of them being housewives so a easy to use and simple UI might prove to be really helpful)

Merchant App

The app for the Merchant user type would have a wider variety of operations available, such as the merchant would be able to manage the virtual inventory from the app itself (i.e. stocking or showing availability of different items via the app) and would also have a dedicated section to keep a track of all the orders being placed. The order tracking section would be very comprehensive in the aspect that it would enable the merchant to keep track of order that he has received but has yet to process, as well as the orders that are under process/out for delivery. The order tracking and virtual inventory management system together would also help in easing the burden of calculating the total sales and profits earned as everything would have been cross referenced in terms of being added to the virtual inventory as an available item, and then being sold at a certain amount of price.
