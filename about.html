Digitizing & mobilizing economy of 600,000 Indian villages & mass job creation around the new rural-urban connected economy.

Gramkalyan.com (GK) is a web portal started with a vision to digitise indian villages. We at GK plan to help farmers and artizens from villages and art clusters get access to global and Indian urban markets leveraging technology.

We expect to set TFC, trade facilitation centers in villages to ensure villagers and artizens have a goto place to push their produce on to our platform. Furthermore we expect the TFCs to be run by the local talent who is web savy and will be trained by the GK experts online as well as physical centers planned in some of the centers of excellences plannes in few citis across India.

One can also see now a days due to digitization the new products and best offers/knowledge on day to day products is available mererly to urban population, with TFC we also envision that the same supplies will be available to villages with assisted model locally in your own villages or clusters.

Gramkalyan founders and teams are seasoned experts from the industry who have come together from different fraternities and streams with the intent to build and scale the Rural economy of India leveraging digitization, global institutional connect and rural manpower.

Last but not list GK is an enabler than owner of any of the trade you do, knowledge you seek or gain. Our vision is to set guiding rails and pies helping trade as well as knowledge access to villages.
